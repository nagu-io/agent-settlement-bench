# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project is committed to constructive, technical collaboration.

Expected behavior:
- discuss ideas and evidence, not people
- provide actionable feedback
- assume good intent and ask clarifying questions
- keep discussions relevant to the benchmark and its outputs

Unacceptable behavior:
- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- spam, trolling, or repeated bad-faith disruption
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this policy.
Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent participation restrictions.

## Reporting

To report conduct issues, open a private maintainer contact through GitHub (not a public issue).
